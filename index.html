Dr. Marc Alber

The minute you walk through the door, you will know you've come to the right place. Dr. Alber combines state-of-the-art dental techniques with a gentle, friendly style that will make you and your family more comfortable than you ever thought possible.

Dr. Alber earned his doctorate from the Creighton University School of Dentistry in Omaha, Nebraska, and each year he continues to study the latest advancements in clinical dentistry. His use of the latest advances in technology - including CEREC one-visit restorations, digital x-ray technology, and conscious sedation - will help you and your family maintain beautiful smiles for a lifetime.

Best of all, Dr. Alber listens and gets to know each patient's unique dental needs. It's the best of both worlds - today's advanced dentistry and old-fashioned personalized care - and it really is something to smile about!

He and his wife, Nikki, have two children and enjoy being an active part of our local community.

Jo

Jo joined our team as Front Office Manager in March of 1999 after moving to Boulder from the California bay area. With over 10 years of experience in the dental field she will happily assist you in making your dental experience a pleasant one. Generally, Jo is the voice you'll hear on the phone when scheduling your visit & she'll be the first smiling face you see upon entering the practice. In addition to scheduling, she will assist you with insurance billing and financial arrangements. In her free time she enjoys reading, skiing and camping with her husband & children.

Meggie

Meggie joined out team in 1999 after moving to Boulder from Germany with her husband & 3 children. She began her career as a Dental Hygienist in 1979 in Florida and has practiced in Germany and Switzerland as well. Meggie is an asset to our team with her extensive experience & dependable nature. She along with her family enjoy staying active with a variety of activities including running, swimming and hiking to name a few.

Monica

Monica joined our team in 2004 for the second time. We are thrilled to have her back as she brings with her a contagious humor and 15 years of experience in the dental field as a chair side assistant. Monica is a Colorado native and enjoys reading, quilting and home repair projects in her free time.

Services We Offer

Click on any service below to learn more about our offerings.

Family Care

Family Care


From toddlers to the elderly, our skilled dental team is familiar with the unique needs of every patient, no matter their age. Our goal is to help them develop great oral habits that will last them a lifetime.

Prevention & Gum Care

Prevention & Gum Care


By actively preventing disease and decay through regular home care, professional dental cleanings and regular exams, you will maintain a healthy, beautiful smile for life.

Digital Radiography

Digital Radiography


Our use of digital x-ray technology reduces your radiation exposure and eliminates the use of chemicals. It’s better for you and the environment.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Cosmetic Dentistry


Cosmetic dentistry is one of the most cost-effective ways to improve your appearance. A beautiful, bright, healthy smile can increase self confidence and overall life satisfaction.

Implant Dentistry

Implant Dentistry


Dental implants are a great option for replacing missing teeth, improving chewing ability, eliminating bridges, and replacing dentures. Call us today for a complimentary implant consultation.

Sedation Dentistry

Sedation Dentistry

Sedation Dentistry

Sedation dentistry is an excellent treatment for patients with extensive dental needs, or who are concerned about anxiety, difficulty getting numb, or sensitive teeth.

One-Visit Crowns

One-Visit Crowns

CEREC® is the world's only system for the fabrication of all ceramic dental restorations in one office visit. With CEREC®, we can complete your permanent, all ceramic crown in just one quick visit! This means fewer shots, less drilling, and less time out of your hectic schedule for dental care.

Invisalign® Orthodontics

Invisalign® Orthodontics


These “invisible” aligners are the latest in orthodontic technology for adults and teens. Virtually undetectable, get the beautiful smile you’ve always wanted.

Root Canal Therapy

Root Canal Therapy


Root canal therapy is an excellent way to save a tooth that would otherwise die and need to be removed.

Porcelain Crowns & Bridges

Porcelain Crowns & Bridges


Protect and keep badly decayed or fractured teeth while maintaining a beautiful smile. Made to perfectly match the shape and shade of your other teeth, they’re durable, strong, and undetectable.

ZOOM!® Smile-Whitening

ZOOM!® Smile-Whitening


Many everyday things like coffee, tea and wine can significantly dull teeth. Zoom! Whitening enables us to significantly brighten your smile in just one visit.

Cosmetic Bonding

Cosmetic Bonding


Composite resin is a white, tooth colored material that can be shaded to match your teeth. This material can give you a low-cost improvement in your smile.
